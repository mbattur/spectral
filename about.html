About

Learning to code can be a lonely endeavor. The problem you're facing can seem like very unique that there is no answer on Stack Overflow or Google. Amount of information one needs to observe can feel like too much and growing more with every updates and new technologies. Or sometimes you just want to talk to someone who is going through same thing as you are. Here at CodeWasabi we are aiming to be that place, where developers can share their knowledge, wisdom, tricks and many more.

Here you can learn about developers around the world at different skill levels. We will dive into problems they're facing, projects they are working and technologies they are excited about. It's a gathering place where information and encouragements can be found about all things developing.
